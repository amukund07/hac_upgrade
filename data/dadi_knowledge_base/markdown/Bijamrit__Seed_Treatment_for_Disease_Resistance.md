## 📖 Summary

Bijamrit is the companion to Jeevamrit — a seed treatment that coats seeds before planting with a protective microbial layer. It dramatically reduces seed-borne disease while activating beneficial soil microbes from the first moment of germination.

## 🌿 Ingredients

- Cow Dung: 500g
- Cow Urine: 1L
- Water: 5L
- Lime: 50g
- Desi Cow Dung (fresh)
- Desi Cow Urine
- Native Soil

## 📋 How to Use

1. Dissolve 50g lime in 5L water overnight
2. Add 500g fresh cow dung + 1L cow urine
3. Add handful of native soil
4. Mix and allow to ferment for 24 hours
5. Filter through cloth
6. Pour over seeds on a clean surface, coat thoroughly
7. Shade-dry for 30 minutes
8. Plant immediately — do not store Bijamrit-treated seeds

## 💚 Health Benefits

Seed Germination, Disease Resistance, Seedling Vigour, Root Development

## 🧬 Scientific Backing

- Reduces damping-off disease (fungal) by 60-80% vs untreated seeds
- Improves germination percentage by 15-20% in field trials
- Lime creates pH barrier against soil-borne pathogens
