## 🌺 Origin Story

In the Ramayana, when Rama's army of monkeys was killed in battle, the celestial being Indra sprinkled nectar over the battlefield. Where this nectar fell on the dead, the Giloy creeper sprouted — giving it the name 'Amrita' — immortality.

## 📋 How to Use

1. Giloy kadha: boil 2-inch fresh giloy stem in 2 cups water, reduce to 1 cup, strain
2. Add tulsi and black pepper for enhanced respiratory benefit
3. Giloy juice: blend fresh stem + water, strain, drink 20ml on empty stomach
4. For dengue/chikungunya: giloy juice 20ml twice daily increases platelet count
5. Giloy churna: 1/4 tsp powder with honey twice daily for immunity maintenance
6. For arthritis: giloy + ginger decoction, reduces joint inflammation significantly
