Bhai, yeh sun — yeh sirf purani baatein nahi, yeh science hai... Dhanvantari is the Hindu god of medicine who emerged from the churning of the cosmic ocean (Samudra Manthan) holding the pot of Amrita (immortality nectar). He is the divine founder of Ayurveda. The Dhanvantari Trayodashi festival (Dhanteras) celebrates his birthday — the day before Diwali. — The secret: our ancestors were not superstitious — they were scientists without the vocabulary.

## 📖 Summary

Dhanvantari is the Hindu god of medicine who emerged from the churning of the cosmic ocean (Samudra Manthan) holding the pot of Amrita (immortality nectar). He is the divine founder of Ayurveda. The Dhanvantari Trayodashi festival (Dhanteras) celebrates his birthday — the day before Diwali.

## 🌺 Origin Story

Dhanvantari is mentioned in the Bhagavata Purana as the twelfth avatar of Vishnu, who descended to end the suffering of disease and death. The Charaka Samhita and Sushruta Samhita both trace their lineage through him.
