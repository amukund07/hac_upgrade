## 📖 Summary

Shilajit is a sticky resinous substance exuded from Himalayan rocks formed over millennia from compressed plant matter. It contains 85+ minerals in ionic form, fulvic acid, and humic acid. Ayurveda calls it 'Yoga Vahi' — the carrier that amplifies the benefits of every herb taken with it.

## 🌿 Ingredients

- Shilajit (resin or powder)
- Warm Water or Milk
- Honey

## 📋 How to Use

1. Dissolve pea-sized amount of pure shilajit resin in warm water or milk
2. Add honey to taste, drink in morning on empty stomach
3. Start with small amount and increase gradually over 2 weeks
4. For maximum absorption: take with fat (milk with ghee)
5. Powder form: 300-500mg capsule with warm milk twice daily
6. Cycle: use for 6-8 weeks, then 2 weeks off for best results
7. WARNING: Only use lab-tested, pure shilajit — heavy metal contamination is a real risk in adulterated products
