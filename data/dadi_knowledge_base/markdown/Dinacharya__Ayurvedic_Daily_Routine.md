## 📋 How to Use

1. Brahma Muhurta wake-up: Rise 90 min before sunrise (~4:30-5:30 AM)
2. Ushapan: Drink 2-3 cups warm water (copper vessel preferred) immediately on rising
3. Natural elimination: allow 15 minutes for natural bowel movement
4. Tongue scraping: 7-10 backward strokes with copper scraper (removes ama/toxins from tongue)
5. Oil pulling (Gandusha): 1 tbsp sesame oil, swish 15-20 min, spit in bin
6. Abhyanga: Warm oil self-massage head to toe before shower (15-20 min)
7. Vyayama (Exercise/Yoga): Surya Namaskar + pranayama during Kapha time (6-10 AM)
8. Breakfast: light, warm, after exercise — not immediately on waking
9. Main meal (Dinacharya): at noon (Pitta peak — strongest digestive fire)
10. Nap: 20-30 min post-lunch in summer (Nidra — not in winter)
11. Evening walk at sunset — Sandhyavandana time
12. Dinner: light, before 7 PM — do NOT eat after 8 PM
13. Wind-down: screen-free by 9 PM, sleep by 10 PM (Pitta time begins — avoid sleep at Pitta time)
