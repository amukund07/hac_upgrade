## 📋 How to Use

1. Boil 5g Kabasura Kudineer churna (powder) in 200ml water
2. Reduce to 100ml (half) by continuous boiling
3. Strain and drink warm
4. Take twice daily before meals
5. Treatment course: 7-14 days for active infection
6. Prophylactic: 30ml once daily during peak season
