## 📖 Summary

Amla (Indian Gooseberry) contains the highest natural Vitamin C of any food — 600-700mg per 100g, nearly 20 times more than oranges. Unlike synthetic Vitamin C, amla's tannins protect the ascorbic acid even during cooking. Considered the most important rasayana fruit in all of Ayurveda.

## 🌺 Origin Story

The Dhanvantari Nighantus describes Amla as 'Divya Phala' — divine fruit. The legend says Brahma created Amla from his tears of compassion that fell to earth. Chyawanprash, the 2600-year-old health supplement, is built on amla as its primary ingredient.

## 📋 How to Use

1. Fresh amla juice: blend 2-3 amla with 1/4 cup water, strain, drink with honey
2. Amla powder: 1 tsp in warm water every morning (preserves all nutrients)
3. Amla murabba: candied amla in jaggery — traditional Rajasthani preservation method
4. For hair: boil dried amla in coconut oil until charred, cool, massage scalp — reverses greying
5. For eye health: amla juice + honey 1:1 ratio, 1/4 tsp taken daily
6. For diabetes: amla juice + bitter gourd juice (50:50), 30ml morning empty stomach
