## 🌺 Origin Story

King Amarasakti of Mahilaropya had three 'incorrigible' princes who refused to learn. He gave them to the scholar Vishnu Sharma, who promised to make them wise in 6 months using only stories. The Panchatantra is that curriculum — the world's first 'gamified' teaching method.
