## 📖 Summary

Shatavari means 'she who possesses a hundred husbands' — reflecting its role as the primary Ayurvedic herb for women's reproductive health across all life stages. It is a powerful phytoestrogen, galactagogue (increases breast milk), and adaptogen that supports hormonal balance.

## 🌿 Ingredients

- Shatavari Powder
- Warm Milk
- Honey
- Ghee

## 📋 How to Use

1. Shatavari milk: 1/2 tsp powder in warm milk with honey, take daily
2. For menopausal symptoms: 500mg capsule twice daily for 3 months
3. For milk production: 1/2 tsp shatavari + 1/4 tsp vidarikanda in warm milk twice daily
4. For menstrual irregularity: shatavari + ashoka + lodhra combination (consult vaidya)
5. For fertility: shatavari + ashwagandha in equal parts, 1/4 tsp each in milk

## 💚 Health Benefits

Menstrual Health, Fertility, Menopause, Breast Milk, Hormonal Balance, Immunity, Digestion

## 🔬 Active Compounds

- Steroidal Saponins (Shatavarins I-IX)
- Isoflavones
- Alkaloids
- Mucilage

## 🧬 Scientific Backing

- Galactagogue: Significantly increases prolactin and milk volume in lactating mothers
- Phytoestrogenic: Shatavarin I binds estrogen receptors — manages menopausal symptoms
- Adaptogen: Comparable to ashwagandha in HPA-axis modulation
- Anti-ulcerogenic: Comparable to ranitidine in gastric ulcer protection

## ⚠️ Contraindications & Safety

- ⚠️ Caution with estrogen-sensitive conditions (breast cancer history)
- ⚠️ May interact with diuretics
- ⚠️ Avoid with kidney disease
