## 🌺 Origin Story

The Dhanur Veda (Science of Archery and Combat) describes 107 Marma points — strike them in combat to incapacitate or kill; stimulate them in therapy to heal. The Kerala tradition of Kalaripayattu preserves both applications — the combat system and its healing counterpart.
