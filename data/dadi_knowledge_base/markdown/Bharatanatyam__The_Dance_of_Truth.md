## 🌺 Origin Story

Lord Nataraja (Shiva as the cosmic dancer) is the deity of Bharatanatyam. The Natya Shastra describes 108 karanas (movement units) of Nataraja's cosmic dance — each is a complete yoga posture and expression combined. Devadasis (temple dancers) preserved this art form for millennia in Tamil Nadu temples.
