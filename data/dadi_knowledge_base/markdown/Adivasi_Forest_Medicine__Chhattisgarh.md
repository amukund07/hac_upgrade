## 🧓 Dadi's Story

Sit down child, let Dadi tell you something important... The Adivasi communities of Chhattisgarh have documented over 1200 medicinal plants in their oral tradition. The Baiga, Gond, and Saura tribes use plants unavailable in mainstream pharmacopeias. Their knowledge of forest ecologies and plant combinations represents a unique pharmaceutical resource threatened by deforestation and cultural loss. — They didn't need labs. They had 5000 years of careful observation.
