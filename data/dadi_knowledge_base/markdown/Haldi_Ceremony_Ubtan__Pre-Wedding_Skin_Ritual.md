## 🌿 Ingredients

- Turmeric
- Sandalwood Powder
- Rose Water
- Chickpea Flour (Besan)
- Milk
- Honey
- Saffron
- Neem

## 📋 How to Use

1. Mix 2 tbsp besan + 1 tsp turmeric + 1 tsp sandalwood + rose water into smooth paste
2. Optional: add saffron strands soaked in 2 tbsp milk + 1 tsp honey
3. Apply to face and body in circular motions
4. Allow to dry 15-20 minutes
5. Exfoliate gently with hands under water — removes dead skin while tightening pores
6. Use daily for 1 week before a special occasion for visible brightening
7. For daily face care: besan + turmeric + yogurt paste, rinse after 10 min

- Turmeric curcumin: Inhibits melanin synthesis (same target as hydroquinone — without side effects)

- Sandalwood: Inhibits 5-alpha-reductase — anti-acne mechanism
- Rose water: Tannins reduce pore size and inflammation
