## 📋 How to Use

1. Idli-Dosa batter: soak rice + urad dal separately 6 hours, grind, ferment overnight (8-12 hours)
2. Kanji (Probiotic Drink): grate carrots/beetroot, soak in mustard water 2-3 days at room temperature
3. Lassi: blend yogurt + water + spices — naturally probiotic, digestive, cooling
4. Chaas (Buttermilk): diluted yogurt with rock salt + roasted cumin — post-meal digestive
5. Pickle: seasonal vegetables in oil + mustard seed + fenugreek — traditional lacto-fermentation
6. Koozh (Tamil Nadu): fermented millet porridge — overnight soak, morning meal

## 💚 Health Benefits

Gut Health, Immunity, Digestion, Nutrition, Mental Health, Diabetes, Cholesterol

## 🧬 Scientific Backing

- Fermentation increases B-vitamin content by 200-300% (especially B12 in yogurt-based)
- Phytic acid reduction: 60-90% reduction in antinutrients during idli/dosa fermentation
- Gut diversity: Daily fermented food consumption increases microbiome diversity by 20% (Stanford RCT)
- Lactobacillus plantarum from Indian pickles: Clinically proven probiotic strain
