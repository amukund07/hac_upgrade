## 🌿 Ingredients

- Sesame Oil (Vata)
- Coconut Oil (Pitta)
- Mustard Oil (Kapha)

## 📋 How to Use

1. Choose oil based on dosha: sesame for Vata, coconut for Pitta, mustard for Kapha
2. Warm oil to comfortable temperature (test on inner wrist)
3. Begin at scalp — circular motions, fingertips pressing gently
4. Move to ears, neck, shoulders with gentle circular strokes
5. Chest and abdomen: clockwise circles (following direction of digestion)
6. Arms and legs: long strokes from joint to joint, circles at joints (wrists, elbows, knees, ankles)
7. Feet: thorough massage including soles and between toes
8. Rest for 15-20 minutes allowing oil to absorb
9. Shower with warm water, minimal soap to preserve oil benefits
